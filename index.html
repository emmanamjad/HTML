Scrambled Eggs

eggs are one of my favourite food . here is a recipie for deliciously rich scrambled egg

Ingredients
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method
  1. melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 second
